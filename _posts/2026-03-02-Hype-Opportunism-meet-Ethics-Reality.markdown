The big news this week was the blacklisting of Anthropic by the Department of War, due to their refusal to give on their redlines around mass surveillance and autonomous (no human in the loop) weapons. Not too surprisingly, Altman then swooped in and struck a deal for OpenAI, that purported to get the deal that Anthropic couldnt, but actually doesnt. OpenAI's opportunism soft of fits with their usual MO.

This is of course the issue you always face when you have a dual use technology. Exactly how do you marry the peaceful and military use cases, and how do you manage against the abuse. Anthropic are quite clear that they dont believe that the technology is currently mature enough to use for autonomous weapons (we should probably get to self-driving cars first), but the DOW wants to reserve that judgement for themselves. The reality is that there will be opportunistic providers that will work around those concerns if there are $$s at stake, as we've seen with OpenAI, and so you have to negotiate in such as way as to keep a seat at the table so you can continue to influence the outcomes. Being outside the conversation but righteous, actually doesnt achieve the aim of limiting the risks. This story has more to go, but this is reality and ethics colliding, in a not surprising way.

In other news OpenAI scored more funding and managed to get a big chunk of that funding from Amazon who have seized on the opportunity to play catchup, though in a strange way, given anything they build with OpenAI (stateful agents?) will still rely on models running on Azure. So funding that goes to OpenAI to hand to microsoft? Strange world.

In an "only the paranoid survive" move, Nvidia is rumored to be building/releasing an inference targetted chip built from the Groq technology they acquired to be announced in the next couple of weeks. And in other news Meta is spending money with everyone, buying optionality at the expense of its in house chip development efforts. 

Busy week for sure, but thats not unusual at all in the ai world at the moment, and Deepseek still are rumored to have a new model about to be released. So also much to look forward to as well.

Oh and a war broke out.
