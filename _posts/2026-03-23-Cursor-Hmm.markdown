Following on from last week, when i posited that folks other than OpenAI, Anthropic and Google would find it hard to stay relevant in the GenAI software engineering space, there was reporting today in "The Information" of real defection(s). They reported that Notion was moving away from Cursor towards OpenAI code and Claude Code. That fits with my general impression of how things are moving. They also reported on an unforced error by Cursor that put them in a bad light. As i mentioned last week, Cursor were nominally differentiated from some of the other players in the IDE based GenAI assist space by their creation of their own models, competitive with the frontier labs. Well it turns out, not so much. It came to light that their new model, Composer 2, which they implied was all their own work, was actually derived from Kimi 2.5. Took them a few days to admit it. Which was a knock on their credibility, and i think reinforces the theme that they are struggling to stay relevant in the face of the push from the frontier model creators. Its going to be interesting, now, to see how this plays out. But clearly the whole thing has been a knock on their reputation.
